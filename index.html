Easy-to-access, top quality services.

Eagle Cove Bar Services stands dedicated to providing easy-to-access, top-quality, and prompt support and assistance to all law firms, ranging from solo practitioners to large, multi-office firms and bar associations, to increase your effectiveness, efficiency, and profits through our guaranteed support and services. We are driven to empower you and your business or Association, so you achieve all your goals. Eagle Cove Bar Services enables you to focus on your key deliverables with our back-office support.

What We Offer

Back-Office Support (Nationwide)

Eagle Cove Bar Services offers you back office, including (A) secretarial supportfrom a full-service telephone call management to an email to a court to alert the judge that you will be delayed, to client communications, to document production; (B) paralegal and paraprofessional support including preparing summonses and other routine documents, divorce submission packages, and proofreading lawyer-prepared materials; (C) nationwide service of process with timely returns and filings of proofs of service.

Our Legal Research Division will customize a quote for you for any brief, research memorandum, affidavit, contract, or other documents, all of which will be prepared by an attorney with your law firm able to capitalize on and profit from the rate your client will pay compared to the cost of this associate-on-demand service.

Our Bar Association Division provides bar associations of all sizes membership management services, newsletter production, special event coordination (including the creation of event journals to enhance events like installation dinners, holiday parties, and one-of-a-kind speaker events). We provide member benefit services, providing discounts for your Associations members, group insurance rates, and special members-only events. We will manage publicizing your Committee or Association meetings, manage your social media presence, and be an invaluable resource regardless of whether your Association has a dedicated staff or runs on the energy of the leadership team of lawyers.

Individualized CLE (Nationwide)

Eagle Cove Bar Services will customize a continuing legal education curriculum for your specific needs to improve your expertise. Using subject matter experts, experienced trainers and teachers, and professional instructional design, Eagle Cove Bar Services tailors your individualized CLE program (from 1-36 hours worth of instruction) to your principal learning modality to achieve maximum impact. Using a variety of one-on-one, small group, and large group presentations, Eagle Cove Bar Services will enhance your knowledge, either by developing additional expertise or learning new areas of the law. All CLE courses will be approved toward your states CLE requirements. 

Our Bar Association Division happily provides an array of CLE programs, including custom programs, for Bar Association members to enjoy. We can program a single CLE event, a series, or any combination your Association wants. All CLE courses will be approved toward your states CLE requirements.

Market Branding (Nationwide)

Lawyers, particularly those in smaller firms, go from one assignment to the next to the next, with little time to devote to establishing a consistent brand identity that appeals to clients. 

Associate-On-Demand

You cant be everywhere at once, and our per diem support team enables lawyers to have an associate-on-demand.

Service of Process (Nationwide)

Our national network of top-quality process servers will get your papers delivered in time, every time, and will return the proof of service directly to you or, upon request, will file it with the court where permitted. Eagle Cove Bar Services keeps you informed of attempts, and successes, so you stay aware and deliver value-added updates to your clients. 

When substitute service is needed, Eagle Cove Bar Services thorough affidavits of due diligence give you what judges look for when granting substituted service motions. 

Too often, process servers get the papers, and eventually get to it, leaving your client wondering why youre not getting the job done. Eagle Cove Bar Services flies higher than those fly-by-nighters who leave you hanging.
